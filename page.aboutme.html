About Me

I am a proud St. Louis native, current Bushwick resident, and satisfied movie pass-holder. I spend my weeks working in financial service and searching for motivation to go to the gym. I spend my weekends volunteering, looking for new places to eat and staying out later than I intend.

Some of my hobbies include golfing, doing NYT crosswords and trivia. I'm always up to learn something new, take a trip or stay in for a lazy Sunday.
